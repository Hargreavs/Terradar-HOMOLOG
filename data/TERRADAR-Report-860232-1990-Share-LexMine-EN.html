METHODOLOGY AND SOURCES

Analysis based exclusively on official public data

TERRADAR uses a proprietary calculation and analysis methodology that evaluates mining processes across multiple dimensions using data extracted exclusively from official Brazilian and international public sources. The scoring, normalization and weighting algorithms were developed and calibrated considering various aspects of mining regulation, risk analysis and market intelligence. The weight structure, classification ranges and fallback rules are TERRADAR intellectual property and are not disclosed in this report.

Risk Score (0-100)

Assesses vulnerabilities across 4 dimensions: geological, environmental, social and regulatory. Lower is better. Ranges: 0-39 low, 40-69 medium, 70-100 high. Proprietary calculation.

Maturity Assessment

5 levels: Exploratory, Initial, Intermediate, Advanced, Mature. Based on phase, documents and permit status.

Opportunity Score (0-100)

Evaluates return potential across 3 dimensions: attractiveness, viability and security. Weights adjusted to investor profile. Higher is better. Proprietary calculation.

Theoretical in-situ value

Estimate with conservative assumptions per hectare. Does not constitute NI 43-101 / JORC. Indicative reference.
